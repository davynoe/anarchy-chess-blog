The Anarchy Chess Project

Anarchy Chess is a purely fictional web project crafted by a single individual. Before you delve into the world of avant-garde chess pieces and quantum-ranking algorithms, it's essential to acknowledge that Anarchy Chess is a creative endeavor with no basis in reality.

It was all fake?!

This website is not a serious chess platform, and the content presented here holds no truth value. It's an exploration of what chess could be if we dared to imagine beyond the boundaries of tradition. The avant-garde pieces, the Stragonov's Elegance Algorithm, the bugs, even the physicist Stragonov himself... literally every information in this website is imagination, designed to add some colors to the classic game.

Anarchy Chess is a solo project, born out of a desire to experiment with web development. It's a digital playground where creativity reigns supreme, and the only rules that apply are the ones we choose to invent for the sake of amusement.

Feel free to enjoy the content, engage in the imaginative aspects, but remember, Anarchy Chess is an idea that is confined by the limits of this webpage.
